IMPORTANT RULES (MASA'EL)
  • Zakaath is obligatory on every muslim(men or women) who posess 612gm of silver or equivalent of cash or any other assets mentioned above, upon the completion of one year.
  • 2.5% of the total amount should be paid as zakaath
  • Zakaath can be paid only to those muslims who do not have 612gms of silver or any other equivalent amount of cash or assets mentioned above
  • It is mentioned in the Quraan, which are as follows: 1. Faqeer(Who does not have food even for a single time). 2. Miskeen (Who has food which is enough only for one time). 3. Muslim Prisoner 4. Indebted 5. In the way of Allah (like Islamic Madarsa Students, etc.,) 6. Wayfarer(traveller) if in need.
  • Zakaath can't be paid to Sayyeds, Parents, Grandparents, Children, Grand children, etc. similarly husband can't pay zakaath to his wife and wife to her husband. Zakaath can be given to all relatives other than these
  • Zakaath cannot be paid to Non-Muslims
    • Important Note: Previous year's zakaath which has not been paid till now must be paid immediately.
